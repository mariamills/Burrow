Everything you need to manage secrets

Built for teams that take security seriously

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Every secret encrypted with a unique random nonce. Keys derived via HKDF-SHA256. If someone steals your database, they get ciphertext.

Users, Groups & Roles

Create teams, assign predefined roles, scope access by namespace. Your backend team reads production secrets. Your intern reads staging. Nobody reads both.

Secret Rotation & TTLs

Rotate secrets manually or on a schedule. Set expiration dates with warnings. Every old value is versioned and retrievable. Nothing gets lost underground.

Shamir Unsealing

Split the master key across your team. No single person can unlock the vault alone. 3-of-5 threshold unsealing, just like the big vaults do.

Full Audit Trail

Every read, write, delete, and failed login is logged. Who accessed what, when, from where. Append-only. Tamper-evident. Your compliance team will love you.

Single Binary Deploy

Pure Go. SQLite included. No Redis, no Postgres required (but supported). Download, set two env vars, run. Your vault is live in under a minute.

Running in 30 seconds

Two env vars. One command. You're live.

# Generate your secrets
export BURROW_ENCRYPTION_KEY="$(openssl rand -base64 48)"
export BURROW_ROOT_TOKEN="$(openssl rand -base64 32)"

# Start the vault
go run ./cmd/burrow

# Store your first secret
curl -X POST localhost:8080/v1/secrets/prod/DB_PASS \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $BURROW_ROOT_TOKEN" \
  -d '{"value": "s3cur3-p4ssw0rd"}'

# Read it back (decrypted)
curl localhost:8080/v1/secrets/prod/DB_PASS \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $BURROW_ROOT_TOKEN"

# Open the dashboard
open http://localhost:8080/ui/
